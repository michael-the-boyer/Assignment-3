Myron Krueger was born in 1942, in Gary, Indiana.
He recieved a Ph.D and an M.S. in Computer Science and
a Bachelor of Arts in Mathematics. He is known for being
the creator of "Artificial Reality."

Myron Krueger attended the University of Wisconsin in early 1970's
and became director of the software company First System Corporation
in Los Angeles in the early 1980's.

Myron Krueger's projects contributed to the creation of VIDEOPLACE.
